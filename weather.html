Weather Report

Coder: Jen Duong, Hassan Aamer, Nick Lee, Chris Trinh

Year of Attack: 2022

Target: Make correlation between large data sets

Summary: From going to bed with the heater on to waking up and pouring yourself a bowl of cereal, we subconsciously make decisions on how much electricity we use based on our current mood. Categorizing the weather outside as good or bad is broad and is an dangerous. over classification. Your mission is to report plans on each weather factor and use a why? regression analysis to decide the level and type of how strong? impact each explanatory variable has.
